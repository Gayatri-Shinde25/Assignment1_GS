Different Yoga Asanas

Fish Pose

1. Lie on your back and tuck your hands under your hips.
2. Press into your elbows and arch your back.
3. Lift your chest and let your head gently rest on the floor.
4. Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Cobra Pose

1. Lie on your stomach with hands under your shoulders.
2. Press into your palms and lift your chest.
3. Keep your elbows close to your body.
4. Hold for 15-30 seconds.

Child Pose

1. Kneel on the floor and sit back on your heels.
2. Fold forward and stretch your arms in front.
3. Rest your forehead on the mat.
4. Hold for 1-3 minutes.

Triangle Pose

1. Stand with feet wide apart.
2. Turn your right foot out, left foot slightly in.
3. Extend your right arm down, left arm up.
4. Hold for 30 seconds, then switch sides.

Tree Pose

1. Stand tall with feet together.
2. Place your right foot on your left thigh.
3. Bring hands together in prayer position.
4. Hold for 30 seconds, then switch legs.

Downward Dog

1. Start on your hands and knees.
2. Lift your hips towards the ceiling.
3. Straighten your legs and press your heels down.
4. Hold for 1-3 minutes.

Warrior Pose

1. Stand with legs wide apart.
2. Turn your right foot out, left foot slightly in.
3. Bend your right knee, arms outstretched.
4. Hold for 30 seconds, then switch sides.

Seated Forward Bend

1. Sit with legs extended in front of you.
2. Inhale and lengthen your spine.
3. Exhale and reach forward towards your feet.
4. Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
